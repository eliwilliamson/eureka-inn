  • One Of The First

    Fun Fact #168

    The Historic Eureka Inn was one of the first lots purchased once Tennessee became a state in 1796.

  • Fire Proof

    Fun fact #357

    The Historic Eureka Inn is one of the only original wooden structures still standing because most of the homes/businesses burned down in the fires of the late 1890s.

  • Preserved Originality

    Fun Fact #294

    Despite fires and renovations, the Historic Eureka Inn still has the original hardwood floors from 1797.
